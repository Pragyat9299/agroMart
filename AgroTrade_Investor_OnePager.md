# AgroTrade — Investor One-Pager

## The Opportunity in One Line

India's makhana (fox nut) market is worth INR 9,300 Crore and growing at 9% CAGR — yet there is no organized digital marketplace connecting farmers to buyers.

---

## What We're Building

AgroTrade is India's first B2B digital marketplace for makhana trade. We connect fox nut farmers in Bihar directly with bulk buyers (traders, exporters, D2C brands) — eliminating middlemen, enabling real-time price discovery, and bringing transparency to an unorganized INR 9,300 Crore market.

**Live Product:** https://agro-mart-green.vercel.app

---

## The Problem

Bihar produces 90% of India's makhana across 9 districts in the Mithilanchal region. Despite being the backbone of this industry, farmers face:

- No price transparency — forced to sell to local aggregators at whatever rate is offered
- Farmers receive only 50-55% of the final retail price (rest eaten by 3-4 intermediaries)
- No digital infrastructure — entire trade happens via phone calls and physical mandis
- No quality standardization — grading is arbitrary, disputes are common
- Zero market intelligence — farmers have no visibility into demand or price trends

The Government of India recognized this gap by announcing the National Makhana Board in Union Budget 2025.

## What's Built and Live Today

- Real-time price listings with district-level price comparison
- Direct buyer-farmer ordering with full order lifecycle (Pending > Confirmed > In Transit > Delivered)
- Role-based access: Farmer, Buyer, Admin dashboards
- Commission tracking per buyer with revenue analytics
- Price history audit trail for market intelligence
- Bulk farmer onboarding via Excel upload
- OTP-based authentication, JWT security
- WebSocket-powered live price updates
- Cursor-based pagination for scale
- Mobile-responsive UI

**Tech Stack:** Spring Boot (Java 21) + React + MySQL + Railway + Vercel
